Comparative Stock Peformance
It is difficult based on the limited analytics performed, to determine which stock provides the best investment opportunity. In terms of overall stock price growth, Northrup Grumman has shown the greatest increase going from $51 at the beginning of 2010 to $301 per share at the end of 2020. In contrast, Ratheon Technologies's stock only has gone from $44 to $70 during that time period. However, while the entire sector has shown growth, the rates of growth have been highly different per stock, with significant movement variances for all stocks in the sector's up versus down years. In addition, the entire sector may be overpriced due to the rapid appreciation of the stock market in recent years. Thus, further fundamential analysis of a company's earnings and a technical analysis of its price vs. recent and historical averages, would have to be done before recommending a stock in this sector.
