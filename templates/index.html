Trend Analysis

As seen in the graph above, the NASDAQ Average has shown expontential growth in recent years, and has brought new opportunities for stock investors. However, investing money in today's stock market requires a sophisticated approach to building a stock portfolio. Analysis of indices based on Nasdaq or Dow Jones Industrial Average performance show that the return on diversified investments, such as Exchange Traded Funds (ETFs), could provide higher investment performance than investing solely in a more limited number of stocks. To help investors potentially increase their investment returns, our team has created a series of visualizations for analyzing stock historical performance and potential invesment returns.

The expression “don’t put all your eggs in one basket” is one of the recommended strategies to investing. A diversified portfolio requires an individual to pick multiple categories and evaluate those companies in each category for the best stock. In this Project, we analyzed five similar aerospace stocks to determine which would be the best candidate to add to a diversified portfolio. We compared Boeing (BA), Lockheed (LMT), Northern Grumman (NOC), Raytheon Technologies (RTX), General Dynamics (GD) and Honeywell (HON).
