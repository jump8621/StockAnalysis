Quick Look

A diversified portfolio requires an individual to pick multiple categories and evaluate those companies in that category for the best stock. The expression “don’t put all your eggs in one basket” is one of the keys to investing.

The purpose of this project to compare several similar stocks in one category and determine which one is the best value. In our project, we will be comparing Boeing (BA), Lockheed (LMT), Northern Grumman (NOC), Raytheon Technologies (RTX), and General Dynamics (GD). Phase 1 offers users several visuals allowing our user to compare stocks. Phase 2 will incorporate machine learning with details yet to be determined.
